Moving the blog again

Date Sun 25 February 2024 Category Tech Thoughts Tags blog

My blog has been hosted on Micro.blog since July after I last moved from GitHub pages. Since then, the fediverse has boomed, but I felt cut off in Micro.blog. They don't support likes or easy reblogs (by design) so I eventually split Microblog and Mastodon into two accounts.

Since then I felt like I was neglecting my blog, so here I am back on hosted Wordpress. This should support sending posts out to fediverse, but I still need to understand how that works. I think I also enabled cross-posting.
