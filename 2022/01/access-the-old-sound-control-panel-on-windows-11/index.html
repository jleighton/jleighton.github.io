Access the old Sound control panel on Windows 11

Date Sat 01 January 2022 Category howto

This will give you access to the older sound control panel screens. I needed this as I was trying to access the 'Listen' mode on a capture card/line in device, and couldn't find any other way of doing this in Windows 11.

To access the old 'Sound' control panel screen in Windows 11:

![](/images/2024/02/4da62-566e8f4163.png)

Enter the following: "rundll32.exe shell32.dll,Control_RunDLL mmsys.cpl,,0"

![](/images/2024/02/164b2-cf3e6b6ac2.png)

You can now access the Recording Tab, and set listen mode on your Capture Card.
